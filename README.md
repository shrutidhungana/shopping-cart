# Shopping-cart
An app made using react and regular css. Here, there are products in the home section ranging through different categories and if you click on Add to Cart button, the items moves to the cart section. If you click on the cart you will get the items you added from the homepage. You can increase the items from your cart clicking the plus button and the quantity increases, or decrease it clicking the minus button and the item
decreases, the minus button is disabled when the quantity is 1 as when you add the items to the cart there should be atleast one quantity of the item or remove the items from the cart by clicking Remove from Cart button. You can click on the link below to know more about the app:

https://shrutidhungana.github.io/shopping-cart/
